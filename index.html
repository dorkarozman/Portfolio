ABOUT ME

I'm a second-year graphic design student with a passion for creating bold, impactful visuals. My journey into graphic design stems from my dream of working in the music industry, where I aim to craft merch and branding that captures the essence of artists and their sound.

I draw inspiration from the raw edge of brutalism and the clean sophistication of modernism, blending these styles to create designs that are both striking and timeless.Whether it's experimenting with typography or pushing creative boundaries, I'm always looking for ways to bring stories to life through design.
